The Process

1. INITIAL CONSULTATION

Like any relationship, it all starts with a first date to make sure the sparks fly! I will ask you some questions regarding your business, vision, and goals for your brand and/or web design, as well as allowing you to ask any questions to me. This step will give us both a chance to get to know one another and make sure we are a good fit.

We’ll review your project scope as well as a timeline and pricing so that we can make sure everyone’s on the same page. Once we’ve agreed to work together – your project is placed in my queue.

2. WHAT’S YOUR STYLE – HOMEWORK

Since there is typically a wait time between this step and the next, I give you homework to prepare for our upcoming project.

I’ll send you a questionnaire to complete that will help me figure out the brand style you’re seeking. We’ll review typography, colors, and imagery that you feel is in line with your ideal brand aesthetic. This questionnaire will provide valuable information for me as I move throughout your brand process. I also ask you to create a detailed pinterest board, full of branding inspiration (it can be anything from colors, to patterns, outfits, interiors, etc.)

3. BEGINNING THE PROCESS


This is where the party starts! I’ll review your branding questionnaire, along with your inspiration board. The more info submitted to me, the better to make sure I am heading in the right direction.
This next phase is all about merging together the perfect mixture of aesthetics for your brand. You will receive brand inspired color palettes along with typography. Once this is approved by you, we begin the design process.
