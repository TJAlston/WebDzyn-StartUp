The Process

I am a Web Designer and Graphic Designer with a Bachelor's of Arts Degree in Communication Studies from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington and I have a ceritification in Front End Engineering from the Iron Yard. My high school education was at Cooperative Arts and Humanities High School in New Haven CT with a concentration in Visual Arts.


Web Designing and Graphic Designing has been a passion of mine for over 20 years. I was first introduced to HTML/CSS during the era of myspace. Once I started designing my own page other people would request for me to change their pages and next thing I knew I was building webpages on angelfire for a sorority, then I was hired as a Marketing Director for a Credit Union in North Carolina in my early 20’s because of it.


In the 2010’s I started working with marketing agencies. My daily clietele included global social media companies and law firms, concert venues all over the United States, and Ivy League schools. I’ve worked with my cleints first hand with their website designs and updates, logo designs and adjustments, apparel designs and marekting campaings.


That blended with my creative mind made inspired me to create Web Dzyn. I wanted to share my skill set with my comminuty. Specifically starts up and comapnies that need help rebranding from someone that can offer modern and whimsical designs at an affordable cost.
